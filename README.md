## About

**S.N.S (Swillnspin)** is a premium car care shop based in Alexandria, Egypt, offering car wash, detailing, ceramic coating, window tinting, and paint protection film (PPF) services for all vehicle types.

This repository holds the plan and build for **S.N.S's official website** — a platform where customers can browse services, see transparent pricing based on their vehicle size, book appointments, and choose flexible payment options, while the shop manages everything from a dedicated admin dashboard.

📄 Full product plan: [`idea.md`](./idea.md)

---

## ✨ Features

- 🚗 **Vehicle-based pricing** — prices adjust automatically for sedans, SUVs, and trucks/vans
- 🧽 **Full service menu** — car wash, interior detailing, ceramic coating, PPF, and tinting
- 📅 **Live booking calendar** — customers pick an available time slot instantly
- 🚙 **Drop-in or pickup/drop-off** — book the way that suits the customer
- 💳 **Flexible payment** — pay in person, on delivery, or split PPF jobs into installments
- 📸 **Before & after gallery** — real transformations, real proof
- ⭐ **Customer reviews** — social proof built right into the site
- 🛠️ **Admin dashboard** — manage services, pricing, bookings, and customers in one place
- 👥 **Staff access** — separate logins for receptionist and technician roles
- 🌐 **Bilingual** — English and Arabic, with a language switch
